About me

Anand Bagmar

Founder, Software Quality Evangelist

Lives in Pune, India
  • Creator of TTA, WAAT and TaaS
  • Creator of vodQA
  • Selenium Conference Committee Member

Anand is a hands-on and result-oriented Software Quality Evangelist with 20+ years in the Software field. He is passionate about shipping a quality product, and specializes in building automated testing tools, infrastructure and frameworks. Anand writes testing related blogs and has built open-source tools related to Software Testing – WAAT (Web Analytics Automation Testing Framework), TaaS (for automating the integration testing in disparate systems) and TTA (Test Trend Analyzer).
