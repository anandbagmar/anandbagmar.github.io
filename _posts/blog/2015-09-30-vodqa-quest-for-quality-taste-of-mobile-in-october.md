There are 2 vodQA editions coming your way in October 2015.   


##  Quest for Quality - ThoughtWorks, Gurgaon - Saturday, 17th October, 2015

ThoughtWorks Gurgaon is happy to announce 8th edition of vodQA happening on 17th October, 2015 at TW Gurgaon office.

  


The theme for this edition is "**[Quest For Quality](<http://twggn.github.io/vodqa/>)** ", i.e. anything and everything related to quality. The speaker registrations are now open, request you all to submit talks [here](<http://twggn.github.io/vodqa/speaker.html>).

 

##  Taste of Mobile - ThoughtWorks, Chennai - Saturday, 31st October, 2015 

Welcoming speakers for the 6th edition of [VodQA - Chennai](<http://twchennai.github.io/VODQA-Chennai/>). 

This edition of VodQa will give you the 'TASTE OF MOBILE'. We will discuss new practices and ideas in the field of mobile testing.  
You could present your thoughts as a hands-on workshop, a short lightning talk (\~10 mins) or a detailed presentation (\~30 mins), submit your talks [here](<http://twchennai.github.io/VODQA-Chennai/speaker.html>).
