Over the past few years, after having spoken in a lot of individuals & teams in conferences & organizations, I realized the understanding of Agile and what does it mean to do effective Testing on Agile projects / teams is very poor.  
  
So, we at [ThoughtWorks, Pune](<https://www.thoughtworks.com/contact-us>), as part of _**[vodQA Pune - Agile Testing Workshop](<http://essenceoftesting.blogspot.com/search/label/vodQA>)**_ , start 2016 with the objective of connecting with our peers in the Software industry to discuss and understand - "_What is Agile and what does it mean to Test on Agile projects / teams?_ "  
  
We have planned and organized this vodQA conference in a very Lean and simple way - announcement, registrations, agenda and updates - all directly from our [vodQA group](<https://www.facebook.com/events/1129948243683193/>) in [facebook](<https://www.facebook.com/groups/vodqa/>).  
  
This edition of vodQA will be held on _**Saturday, 9th January, 2016 at the ThoughtWorks, Pune office on the 4th floor**_. For more details, see the [vodQA event page](<https://www.facebook.com/events/1129948243683193/>) in facebook.
