A busy couple of weeks for me as I talk about TaaS in [World Conference on Next Generation Testing ](<http://www.nextgentesting.org/>)in Bangalore, on 11/12th July 2013, and in [Agile 2013](<http://agile2013.agilealliance.org/>) in Nashville, USA, on 4-9th August 2013.  
   
My session details are as follows:   
  
In WCNGT - [Integrated Testing In Enterprises Using TaaS](<http://www.nextgentesting.org/agenda/popup/t8054>), on 11th July, from 12pm-1pm. Detail program is available [here](<http://www.nextgentesting.org/program>).
