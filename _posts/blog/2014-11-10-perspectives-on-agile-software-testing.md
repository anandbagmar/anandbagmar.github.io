Inspired by Selenium's [10th Birthday Celebration](<http://www.thoughtworks.com/insights/blog/happy-10th-birthday-selenium>), a bunch of ThoughtWorkers have compiled an anthology of essays on testing approaches, tools and culture by testers for testers.     
This anthology of essays is available as an ebook, titled - "[Perspectives on Agile Software Testing](<http://info.thoughtworks.com/ebook-agile-software-testing>)" which is now available for download from [here](<http://info.thoughtworks.com/ebook-agile-software-testing>) on [ThoughtWorks](<http://www.thoughtworks.com/>) site. A simple registration, and you will be able to download the ebook.
