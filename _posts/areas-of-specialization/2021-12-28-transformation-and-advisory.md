<div class="eot-ta-section">

  <p class="eot-ta-lead">Quality transformation is not a one-size-fits-all exercise. Organisations differ in maturity, culture, team structure, and delivery context. I work with leadership and engineering teams to assess where they are, define where they need to be, and build a practical path to get there - whether that means a focused advisory engagement, a full transformation programme, or targeted training and upskilling for the team doing the work.</p>

  <span class="eot-eyebrow">ENGAGEMENT MODELS</span>
  <h2 class="eot-ta-h2">How We Work Together</h2>

  <div class="eot-ta-models">

    <div class="eot-ta-model">
      <h3>Advisory Engagement</h3>
      <p>Short-term, focused engagements - typically a few days to a few weeks. Ideal for organisations that need an expert perspective on a specific challenge: a quality assessment, a test strategy review, a roadmap workshop, or a second opinion before a major delivery decision.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="eot-ta-model eot-ta-model--accent">
      <h3>Transformation Programme</h3>
      <p>Longer-term engagements that span weeks to months. Covers the full arc from assessment through roadmap design to implementation support. Suited to organisations undergoing significant change - scaling engineering teams, adopting AI-assisted delivery, or rebuilding quality capability after a period of technical debt accumulation.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="eot-ta-model eot-ta-model--training">
      <h3>Training &amp; Upskilling Programme</h3>
      <p>Structured training delivered to individuals or teams - as standalone programmes or embedded within a broader transformation. Formats include bootcamps, classroom sessions, online workshops, and ongoing coaching. Topics range from automation fundamentals and AI-assisted testing to quality strategy for managers and SDET capability building. Programmes are tailored to your team's existing skills, tools, and delivery context.</p>
      <p style="margin-top:0.75rem"><a href="/areas-of-specialization/trainings-and-workshops/">See all training programmes →</a></p>
    </div>

  </div>
