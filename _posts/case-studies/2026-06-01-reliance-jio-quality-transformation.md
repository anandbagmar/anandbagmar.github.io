## The Challenge

Reliance Jio operates at massive scale — spanning telecom, retail, e-commerce, and media products across millions of users. As delivery velocity increased and product complexity grew, quality engineering needed to evolve from reactive testing to a structured, scalable capability embedded across 20+ product teams.

---

## What Was Delivered

### Assessment & Strategy

Reviewed existing practices across teams, identified bottlenecks and redundancies, and created an actionable quality roadmap aligned with business goals. The assessment surfaced the gap between perceived and actual quality — giving leadership a clear, evidence-based starting point.

### Automation Frameworks

Built reusable test automation frameworks covering:

- **Web** — cross-browser automation with stable, maintainable architecture
- **Mobile** — Android and iOS native app testing (teswiz-based)
- **Desktop** — Windows application automation
- **APIs** — contract and integration testing

All frameworks were integrated with **Azure DevOps quality gates** — shifting quality left into the CI/CD pipeline and providing fast, actionable feedback on every build.

### AI Visual Testing

Implemented AI-driven visual testing across applicable product surfaces — improving the reliability of visual validations at scale and reducing false positives that had been slowing release confidence.

### QECC Establishment

Stood up a **Quality Engineering Competency Centre (QECC)**:

- Standardised tools, frameworks, and processes across teams
- Sourced, recruited, and onboarded 75+ SDETs
- Built competency maps and integrated engineers into active product teams
- Created career pathways and mentoring structures to sustain the capability long-term

---

## Outcomes

| | |
|---|---|
| **Coverage** | Automation across web, mobile, desktop, and API layers |
| **CI/CD Integration** | Quality gates embedded in Azure DevOps pipelines for 20+ teams |
| **Team Scale** | 75+ SDETs hired, onboarded, and integrated |
| **Reliability** | Reduced visual regression false positives via AI-driven testing |
| **Sustainability** | Competency centre with standardised frameworks and clear career structure |
