I am **Anand Bagmar** — a **Software Quality Executive, Keynote Speaker, and Test Automation Expert** with **25+ years** of experience leading quality strategy and transformation across complex technology environments.

I am open to senior quality engineering leadership roles — FTE or fractional/interim — as well as consulting and advisory engagements — bringing strategic perspective, technical depth, and hands-on leadership to improve product confidence, release performance, and engineering effectiveness.

My work spans quality assessments, roadmap definition, automation strategy, and hands-on implementation across telecom, e-commerce, gaming, SaaS, enterprise platforms, and banking/financial services. A Selenium and Appium contributor and creator of **Teswiz**, **WAAT**, and **TTA** — open-source tools used globally — I am also a regular conference speaker and writer on software quality and AI-driven test automation.

> *Strategic where it matters, practical where it counts — helping organisations turn quality into a durable business advantage, whether as a leader, a consultant, or both.*
